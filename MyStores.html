MY STORES¶

The My Stores screen on the platform gives the list of different stores that are accessible to a particular user. Each card depicts the summarized information regarding the store depicting the ‘store name’, ‘store location’, ‘total sales generated’, ‘out of stock count’ and ‘number of alerts’ for the given day.
