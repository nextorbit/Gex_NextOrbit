OUT OF STOCK ANALYSIS¶

The Out of Stock Analysis screen of the platform offers insights and depicts the out of stock trend that can be discovered from POS and inventory data. The default time range for which insights are presented on first load is the current week. This can be changed with the help of the time filter in the top right portion of the screen.

Overview Cards¶

The following metrics are depicted as cards:

  • Total Out of Stock - Total percentage of Out of Stock of the products in the given time range.
  • Products - Number of products that have gone out of stock in the time range provided.
  • Total Sales Loss due to Out of Stock ($) - Loss of sales incorporated due to out of stock in terms of revenue. The unit used is for depiction is American Dollar ($).
  • Out of Stock Rate during Promotion (%) - The rate of Out of Stock of the products during the promotion period in a store in the time range provided.

Graphs¶

Line Graph¶

Delivers a graphical illustration of out of stock trend for the specified products at a specified interval of time.

  • The horizontal axis contains time period and the vertical axis represents unit of measurement ($ or number of items).
  • The icons next to the “zoom” text help in increasing or decreasing granularity of the graph.
